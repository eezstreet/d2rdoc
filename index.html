Welcome to the Diablo II: Resurrected Data Guide! This site documents the data files used by Diablo II: Resurrected, providing detailed descriptions of every field and how they interact with each other and the game engine.

This is a community-maintained project. In addition to the official documentation, you may find Community Notes throughout the guide that provide additional context, corrections, and tips contributed by the modding community.
